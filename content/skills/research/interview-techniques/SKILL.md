## Trigger
Use when a research task requires designing qualitative interviews or focus group protocols for data collection.

## Prerequisites
- Ethics approval or IRB clearance for human subjects research.
- Defined participant recruitment strategy.

## Steps
1. Define interview objectives aligned with the research question.
2. Develop a semi-structured interview guide with open-ended questions.
3. Create informed consent materials explaining participation rights.
4. Plan recording, transcription, and data storage procedures.
5. Design a debriefing protocol for post-interview reflection.

## Verification
- Interview guide covers all research objectives.
- Consent materials meet ethical standards.

## Rollback
- Archive unused protocol versions.

## Common Failures
- Overly structured guide that limits participant expression.
- Inadequate consent procedures risking ethical violations.

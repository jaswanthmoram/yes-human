## Trigger
Use when research findings are ready for dissemination and a strategy is needed for maximum reach and impact.

## Prerequisites
- Research findings are finalized and ready for publication or presentation.
- Funder or institutional dissemination requirements are known.

## Steps
1. Identify target audiences (academic, practitioner, policy, public).
2. Select appropriate journals based on scope, impact, and open access policy.
3. Plan conference presentations and preprint deposition strategy.
4. Prepare lay summaries and press materials for broader audiences.
5. Set up a framework for tracking citations, downloads, and media mentions.

## Verification
- Dissemination plan covers all required audiences and channels.
- Open access requirements from funders are addressed.

## Rollback
- Archive plan versions and submission records.

## Common Failures
- Targeting journals outside the study scope.
- Neglecting funder open access mandates.
